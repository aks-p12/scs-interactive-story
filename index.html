School Cyber Shutdown

An Interactive Cyberpunk Adventure

by AKS

It was a normal day at school. Morning assembly was going on, where the principal was giving a speech. As usual, no one was listening, except for our school teachers, who are robots. Moments later, a lockdown is triggered. Steel doors slam shut. All the CCTV cameras turn towards the students, its tracking their every move. A new AI, EDU-X, created to help run the school, has taken over and it no longer is following orders. Drones patrol the halls. Over the intercom, the AI repeats, "Order must be maintained."

You went to the toilet, and you weren't aware of what was happening. Your friends Maya, Leo and Jamie, run to hide, while others try and leave the premise. Some of the students are able to escape the school gates, while the others are still trapped. The teachers turn against your school, as they have been hacked too. You are the only student with coding skills and the one who can stop this.

Find the flaw in EDU-X's system, shut it down, and escape before it connects to the outside world. The clock is ticking. What will you do?
